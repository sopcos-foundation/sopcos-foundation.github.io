## 1. The Concept: Why Sopcos? (Philosophy)

Current industrial systems focus on "Data"; Sopcos focuses on the **"Verdict."**

When an accident occurs, SCADA logs can tell you **what** happened, but they cannot prove **why** it happened or **who is responsible** with legal certainty.

The core thesis of Sopcos is this: **Speed requires reflex, trust requires record, liability requires signature.**

* **Sopcos is not a SCADA;** it is the notary that audits the SCADA.
* **Sopcos is not an AI project;** it is a management layer that encloses AI within a secure cage.
